Pizza Travia

When did the pizzas first enter India?

Pizzas were introduced to India in the 1980s, but it was not until 1996 that some American fast food chains like Domino’s, Pizza Hut, and Papa John’s set up shop with their pan pizzas. Before that, pizza for most Indians was a bread base topped with tomato ketchup and processed cheese. (Source: Google)
